How It Works

BURN

Burn your $BURN tokens on-chain using any Solana wallet. Each burn is permanently recorded.

COMPETE

Climb the real-time leaderboard. Track your rivals. Every burn updates your position instantly.

WIN

Top burner at round end wins 80% of the prize pool in SOL. Funded by pump.fun creator fees.

Game Rules

1-Hour Rounds
Continuous 24/7 rounds. Each round lasts 60 minutes with possible overtime extensions. The first round begins 30 minutes after token launch.
Pure Burn Ranking
Rankings determined solely by total tokens burned. No luck, no randomness. Whoever burns the most, wins.
80 / 20 Split
Winner receives 80% of the prize pool in SOL. 20% goes to operations and development.
Prize Rollover
If no one burns in a round, the entire prize pool carries over, creating jackpot rounds.
Real-Time Leaderboard
All burns tracked on-chain, displayed live. Fully transparent and verifiable on Solscan.
Min 1,000 $BURN
Each burn transaction must be at least 1,000 $BURN to prevent spam and micro-burn manipulation.

Overtime System

When less than 10 minutes remain and 1st place is overtaken by a 5% or greater margin, the round extends by 5 minutes. This prevents last-second sniping and creates dramatic overtime battles.

Strategy Guide

The Aggressor

Burn big early, establish dominance. Force others to overcommit or give up. High cost, high pressure.

The Sniper

Wait until the final minutes and strike. Risk triggering overtime where opponents can respond, but conserves capital.

The Accumulator

Skip weak rounds, accumulate $BURN. Strike when the jackpot from rollovers is worth the fight.

The Grinder

Consistent participation across rounds. Win the small pots others ignore. Volume over magnitude.
